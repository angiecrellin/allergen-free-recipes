How do I find allergen free recipes?

Living with a food allergy can be challenging. Here you can search for healthy recipes that are free of one or more of the following allergens: Peanut, Treenut, Shellfish, Fish, Milk, Egg, Soy and Wheat.

  • 1. Click on the allergens you want to avoid.
  • 2. Click on the "Search for Recipes" button.
  • 3. Add your own allergen free recipes by clicking "Add a Recipe" and filling in the form.

Enjoy!
